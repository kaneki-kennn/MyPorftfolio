CTU-AC Supply Monitoring System

Cebu Technological University-Argao Campus, in collaboration with Ginatilan Extension Campus, is developing a web-based Supply Request Monitoring System to address inefficiencies in inventory management and communication between various offices, including the Bids and Awards Committee (BAC), Campus Director (CD), and Budget Officer (BO). The current manual tracking method causes delays, miscommunications, and inventory control issues, which affect campus operations and department performance. This automated system will streamline the request submission, approval, and tracking processes, provide real-time updates, and help manage inventory effectively, ensuring timely delivery of supplies and improving overall operational efficiency.

Automated Cooperative System

The Automated Cooperative System makes it easier and faster for people in the Coop to use services. It has a simple design that helps members manage their accounts and get what they need quickly. This system reduces the need for doing things by hand, saves time, and gives members a dependable place to take care of their money and Coop needs.
